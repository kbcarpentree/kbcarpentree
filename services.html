What we build

Each design and build tailored to your life style or anything else can throw at it!

General contracting

Have a vision and need help bringing it to life? Our role is to make this a seamless reality from start to finish. Aiding with virtual walk throughs of your design, contracting and scheduling involved trades, and quality control. Ensuring every detail plays its role in the beauty of your build.

Framing

Including new homes, parking garages, workshops and additions we got you covered. We ensure efficiency and quality from floor to roof. Offering experienced work for the backbone of your build.

Timber Framing

Looking for that wow feature? Timber framing offers creativity highlighting the beauty in nature and history. Inspired by the integrity in quality Canadian lumber and the endless possibilities to transform your space.

Hand-cut Roofs

For that custom look brings everything together. Hand-cut rafters, hips, valleys and dormers with care. A hand-cut roof allows you access to attic storage, maximizing your space.

Decks

Elevate your outdoor entertainment space with elements that implement a functional and stylish area. Specializing in timeless designs with your daily needs in mind.

Garages & Outbuildings

Detached garages, workshops, and storage buildings framed to handle real use! Vehicles, tools, lifts, and whatever else you throw at them.

How the process works

  1. Send us your idea basic details, location, and any drawings you have.
  2. We review and ask questions structural details, design, access, timelines, and budget.
  3. Clear scope & quote we outline our detailed plan for next steps including payment schedule, start date and timeline.
  4. Build we show up on schedule with the tools and the right crew to get it done above your expectations
